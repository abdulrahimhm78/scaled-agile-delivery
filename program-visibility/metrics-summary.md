# Metrics Summary (Executive View)

## Purpose
Metrics summaries provide leadership with
a high-level view of delivery health and
trends without operational noise.

## Metrics Typically Included
- Throughput and delivery trends
- Predictability indicators
- Quality and stability signals
- Dependency and risk trends

## How to Present
- Focus on trends, not snapshots
- Provide context for changes
- Highlight implications and actions

## Common Mistakes
- Sharing raw dashboards
- Overloading executives with detail
- Reporting metrics without interpretation
