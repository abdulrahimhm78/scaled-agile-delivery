# Program Status (Executive Visibility)

## Purpose
Program status provides leadership with
a clear, concise view of delivery progress,
risks, and overall health.

## What to Communicate
- Progress toward objectives
- Key risks and dependencies
- Delivery confidence and trends
- Required leadership decisions

## Good Practices
- Keep reporting concise and factual
- Highlight trends, not noise
- Align status to outcomes, not activity

## Common Mistakes
- Overloading reports with detail
- Hiding risks to appear green
- Reporting activity instead of outcomes
