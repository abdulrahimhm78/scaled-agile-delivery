# Risk Escalation (Timely Intervention)

## Purpose
Risk escalation ensures delivery threats
are addressed at the appropriate level
before they impact outcomes.

## When to Escalate
- Risk likelihood or impact increases
- Team lacks authority to resolve the risk
- Risk threatens PI or program objectives

## Escalation Principles
- Escalate early, not late
- Provide clear context and options
- Focus on resolution, not blame

## Common Mistakes
- Escalating without clarity
- Waiting until the risk becomes an issue
- Escalating too frequently without judgment
