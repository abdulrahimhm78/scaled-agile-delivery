# Risk Identification (At Scale)

## Purpose
Risk identification ensures potential
delivery threats are surfaced early
and addressed proactively.

## Common Risk Areas
- Technical complexity
- External dependencies
- Resource availability
- Regulatory or compliance constraints

## Good Practices
- Identify risks during planning
- Make risks visible to leadership
- Revisit risks regularly

## Common Mistakes
- Treating risks as issues
- Ignoring early warning signs
- Surfacing risks too late
