# Final Plan and Review (Enterprise Delivery Closure)

## Purpose
The Final Plan and Review provides a structured mechanism to formally conclude a delivery cycle, release, or major milestone. It validates outcomes, confirms readiness for closure, captures lessons learned, and ensures enterprise transparency and accountability.

---

## When This Is Used
- End of a release or major initiative
- Completion of a program increment (PI)
- Transition from build to operate
- Executive or governance checkpoint
- Contractual or regulatory milestone closure

---

## Inputs
- Approved Sprint / Release goals
- Definition of Done (DoD) confirmation
- Delivery metrics (velocity, throughput, predictability)
- Risk and dependency status
- Stakeholder feedback
- Retrospective outcomes
- Operational readiness artifacts

---

## Final Delivery Review

### 1. Objectives Validation
- Original objectives and success criteria reviewed
- Delivered outcomes mapped to objectives
- Variances documented with rationale

### 2. Scope and Quality Confirmation
- All committed scope completed or formally descoped
- Definition of Done met for all accepted work
- No open critical defects or unresolved blockers

### 3. Metrics Summary
- Delivery predictability and trend analysis
- Velocity stability and flow health
- Defect escape rate
- Cycle time and throughput indicators

---

## Risk and Dependency Closure

### Risk Status
- All high and medium risks resolved or accepted
- Residual risks documented with owners
- No unmanaged risks remaining

### Dependency Status
- Cross-team dependencies closed or transitioned
- External dependencies formally handed off if required

---

## Operational Readiness Review

### Readiness Checklist
- Documentation complete and accessible
- Support and operational handoff completed
- Monitoring and alerting in place
- Ownership and escalation paths defined
- Knowledge transfer completed where applicable

---

## Stakeholder Review and Sign-Off

### Feedback Summary
- Stakeholder feedback reviewed and acknowledged
- Key insights captured for future planning
- Outstanding concerns documented with follow-up actions

### Approval
- Business and delivery stakeholders confirm acceptance
- Formal sign-off recorded where required

---

## Lessons Learned and Improvements

### What Worked Well
- Practices or behaviors that improved delivery
- Decisions that positively impacted outcomes

### What Needs Improvement
- Constraints, bottlenecks, or recurring issues
- Process or tooling gaps identified

### Actionable Improvements
- Improvement items defined with owners
- Priority and target timeframe established
- Items added to backlog or improvement register
