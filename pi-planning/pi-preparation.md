# PI Preparation (Pre-Planning)

## Purpose
PI preparation ensures teams and stakeholders
enter PI Planning aligned, informed, and ready
to make realistic commitments.

## Key Activities
- Review strategic objectives and priorities
- Validate team capacity and constraints
- Identify high-level dependencies
- Ensure backlog readiness for planning

## Why It Matters
Strong PI preparation reduces planning chaos,
improves alignment, and leads to more predictable
PI execution.

## Common Mistakes
- Treating PI Planning as a one-day event
- Entering planning with unrefined backlogs
- Ignoring known dependencies
